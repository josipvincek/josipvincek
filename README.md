## Research Positioning

Engineering analysis in subsurface systems should be transparent, reproducible, and methodologically rigorous.

My work integrates classical rock mechanics with structured computational approaches to support analytical validation, modelling clarity, and engineering decision processes.

---

## Research Infrastructure

This profile hosts computational frameworks, validation environments, and structured modelling systems supporting ongoing research in geomechanics and underground engineering.
